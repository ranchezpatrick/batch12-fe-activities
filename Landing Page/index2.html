About Paplaya

Paplaya grew out of the vision of one man - Mario Delgado - who defined Asian hospitality and set new standards on luxury and what it means to be a host. Today our service excellence is legendary within the hospitality industry. We are proud of our Asian heritage and strive to become the best-loved hospitality group bringing people together to live, work, play, eat and rest well.
